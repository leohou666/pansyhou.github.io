## jtag 启动步骤

[[中文博客] 通过JTAG在AMD XILINX SoC器件里启动Linux的方法](https://adaptivesupport.amd.com/s/article/000035979?language=zh_CN)

```
connect
targets -set -filter {name =~ "ARM*#0"}
rst -processor
dow zynq_fsbl.elf
con
exec sleep 4
stop
dow -data system.dtb 0x100000
exec sleep 1
dow u-boot.elf
con
dow -data uImage 0x10000000
dow -data system.dtb 0x11000000
dow -data rootfs.cpio.gz.u-boot 0x11A00000   
```
